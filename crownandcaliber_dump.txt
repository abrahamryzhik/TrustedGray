     <h3>
      About Rolex Explorer II Watches
     </h3>
     <p>
      After the retirement of the iconic Rolex
      <a href="/collections/rolex-explorer-watches" title="Shop the Rolex Watch Collection">
       Explorer
      </a>
      model Reference 1016, a new era for the Explorer series began with the introduction of the Explorer II. The Reference 1655 debuted in 1971, and this initial model was specifically designed with speleologists, volcanologists, and polar and jungle explorers in mind. Equipped with some specially designed features, The Explorer II featured a 24-hour bezel and a fourth hand; this hand, which was typically orange, has become a trademark of the model.
     </p>
     <p>
      The Explorer II has a sportier look and feel than the original Explorer, with a newly designed dial, sapphire crystal, and caliber 3000 movement. Everything about the model is bigger and bolder, from the larger case to the lugs and dial markers. The Explorer II didn't really gain attention until after it was rumored to be worn by a legendary actor and racer, leading to the model being nicknamed the "Steve McQueen" Explorer.
     </p>
     <p>
      The Rolex Explorer II is the ideal watch for the avid cave dweller, but you donâ€™t have to be a passionate adventurer to appreciate it. The more rugged design and appearance of the Explorer II makes it a durable and practical watch for everyday wear.
     </p>
